--- v1.0 (Jan 24, 2015)
	- Initial release under GPLv2. 
	- Supports 13 inputs (4 direction + 9 buttons), mapped as follows:	
	   PORTC5: Up
	   PORTC4: Down
	   PORTC3: Left
	   PORTC2: Right
	   PORTC1: Button 0
	   PORTC0: Button 1
	   PORTB5: Button 2
	   PORTB4: Button 3
	   PORTB3: Button 4
	   PORTB2: Button 5
	   PORTB1: Button 6
	   PORTB0: Button 7
	   PORTD7: Button 8
